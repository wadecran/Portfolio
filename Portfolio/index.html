About Me

I'm a full-stack .NET developer with a collaborative style and the drive to take challenges head on.

Problem solving has always been one of my strongest skills, and was originally the main draw to the programming field. In my free time, I enjoy learning whenever the opportunity presents itself, and study with resources such as Udemy and Coder Foundry. Outside of programming I play pool competitively with the American Pool-players Association . The concentration and precision required in such a competition is exactly the type of situations I love to immerse myself in.
